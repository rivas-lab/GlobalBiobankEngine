Downloads

We provide several datasets to the community. Please also check our FAQ page for more information.

Genetics of 35 blood and urine biomarkers in the UK Biobank

The analysis scripts and the summary-level datasets (the meta-analyzed GWAS summary statistics, the results from fine-mapping analysis, the snpnet polygenic risk score models, and the multi-PRS models) used in Genetics of 35 blood and urine biomarkers in the UK Biobank (Sinnott-Armstrong, Tanigawa, et al., bioRxiv 2019; doi:10.1101/660506) are available from GitHub and figshare, respectively.

Rare protein-altering variants in ANGPTL7 lower intraocular pressure and protect against glaucoma

The analysis scripts and the summary-level datasets (GWAS summary statistics of glaucoma and intraocular pressure) used in Rare protein-altering variants in ANGPTL7 lower intraocular pressure and protect against glaucoma (Tanigawa, et al., PLoS Genetics 2020; doi:10.1371/journal.pgen.1008682) are available from GitHub and figshare, respectively. Please also see highlights of the study for more information.

Summary statistics for array meta-analysis

The meta-analysis summary statistics (version 8.15.2020) available in GBE are available for download from Google Drive. The ICDinfo file, i.e. the file mapping the GBE IDs to the phenotype name is available for download from Github.

Genetic correlation across phenotypes in UK Biobank

The analysis scripts used in Assessing Digital Phenotyping to Enhance Genetic Studies of Human Diseases (DeBoever, Christopher, et al. "Assessing digital phenotyping to enhance genetic studies of human diseases." The American Journal of Human Genetics 106.5 (2020): 611-622.) are available from GitHub. The table of the genetic correlation estimated from UK Biobank are available for Download in Github public resources.

Polygenic risk modeling with latent trait-related genetic components

Below are downloads corresponding to "Polygenic risk modeling with latent trait-related genetic components" (Aguirre, et. al.; EJHG 2021). Methods and downloads descriptions for these supplementary files can be found in the manuscript, as well as in the project GitHub. The weights for the model instance described in the manuscript (see below) are also available on figshare.

  • List of analysis phenotypes: degas_n_977_traits.tsv
  • DeGAs model weights: all_beta_center_p1e-06_20200506_500PCs.npz (~0.7GB)
  • Input summary stats: z-stats, betas, p-values, and standard errors (Warning: each file is ~10GB)
  • Model weights from DeGAs on external data: neale-lab-ukbb.degas_beta_p1e-6_500PCs.20201221.npz (~0.9GB)

Please refer to the manuscript and GitHub (links above) for additional information, including performance measures for risk models derived from these data.

Components of genetic associations across 2,138 phenotypes in the UK Biobank highlight adipocyte biology

The analysis scripts and the summary-level datasets used in Components of genetic associations across 2,138 phenotypes in the UK Biobank highlight adipocyte biology (Tanigawa, et al., Nature Communications 2019; doi:10.1038/s41467-019-11953-9) are available from GitHub and figshare, respectively. Please see the DeGAs app page for more information.

Summary-level data from CNV associations

Below are downloads corresponding to "Phenome-wide Burden of Copy Number Variation in UK Biobank" (Aguirre MA, Rivas MA, Priest JR; AJHG 2019, PMC6699064). Description of methods used for analysis and available data can be found in the manuscript and in the readme below.

  • Readme: README.txt
  • List of phenotypes with available data: pheno_index_v2.20190513.txt
  • CNVs Called: cnv.ukb.reference_v2.20190626.txt (~31MB)
  • Summary Stats: cnv.assoc.release_v2.20190513.tar.gz (~1.9GB)
