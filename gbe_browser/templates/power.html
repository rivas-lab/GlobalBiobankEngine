Power application - evaluate empirical power to detect association

Version 0.01

Christopher DeBoever and Manuel A. Rivas

C.D. led the research project and developed the web application. M.A.R designed and supervised the study.

Heads up! This app shows the power to detect genetic associations for collapsed protein-truncating variants (PTVs) in different populations from gnomAD and the UK Biobank (UKB). We estimated collapsed PTV allele frequencies and 95% confidence intervals for each gene that contains PTVs and calculate power using the GeneticsDesign R package. More information on the methods to come. Created by Chris DeBoever (https://cdeboever3.github.io/)
